Bitcoin Trade Volume vs Transaction Volume Ratio

{% include 'bitcoin_trade_volume_vs_transaction_volume_ratio.svg' %}

The Bitcoin Trade Volume vs Transaction Volume Ratio compares the amount of Bitcoin traded on exchanges to the number of transactions conducted on the Bitcoin network. This ratio provides insights into the level of trading activity versus the actual usage of Bitcoin for transactions. A higher ratio may indicate increased speculative trading relative to the number of transactions, suggesting that more Bitcoin is being traded rather than used for payments or transfers. Notably, an extreme value of 224.0653 was observed on 2011-06-20, coinciding with a peak in the Bitcoin price. Additionally, over the past year, the ratio has been increasing, indicating a higher level of speculative trading compared to the number of transactions.
